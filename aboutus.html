WELCOME
TO FOODIE SPOT


Focusing on the gourmet diner as well as the youth of India, Foodie Spot aims to bring Indian cuisine back “in-Vogue”. “Foodie” can have many connotations, but at Foodie spot, it has just one, “creating an illusion” with its cuisine. Best described as a gourmet experience, it amalgamates traditional global and Indian classics, with Indian influences, contemporary presentations, culinary styles and ambiance. It is a quirky, chic, modern Indian café, where guests enjoy a sensory experience through the finest modern Indian cuisine, with a high energy ambiance. Infusing a generous dose of Indian flavours, Foodie Spot presents Indian cuisine where culinary art meets the alchemy of modern presentations and cooking techniques like molecular gastronomy to absorb the guest into the ultimate gastronomic illusion.

CEO AND FOUNDER

R Chockalinga Prabhu

Indulge your senses at Foodie Spot, where the culinary magic unfolds under the watchful eye of our visionary founder, Chockalinga Prabhu R. An alchemist of flavors, Prabhu's journey from global kitchens to the heart of our cafe is a tale of passion and precision. Every cup of coffee and every bite on our menu reflects his commitment to transforming ordinary moments into extraordinary experiences. Join us in savoring the symphony of tastes orchestrated by R Chockalinga Prabhu, as Foodie Spot becomes more than a cafe - it becomes a destination where every visit is a flavorful adventure, curated with expertise and love. Welcome to the gastronomic masterpiece that is Foodie Spot.
